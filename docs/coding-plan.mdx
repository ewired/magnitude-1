The coding plan is $20/mo. 3 day free trial, no card required. Add a payment method to continue with the plan after trial.

## What's included

- **Best models, always.** We benchmark constantly. New model wins, you get it.
- **Verified providers.** Only the ones serving the models correctly and fast.
- **Zero data retention.** Our providers do not train on your code.
- **Built-in web search.** Exa is wired in by default.
- **Generous rate limits.** Sized for full days of agent work. Limits reset every 5 hours.
